Indian Young Physicists League

Indian Young Physicists League (IYPL) is a national theoretical physics competition in which teams will answer challenging theoretical physics questions, designed to both challenges and entertain them.
Conducted every year since 2021, teams of three to four undertake the online exam, IYPL will also be recognised as the national preliminary for PLANCKS (Physics League Across Numerous Countries for Kickass Students), a prestigious annual international competition organised by IAPS that follows a similar format.
The IYPL winning team will represent India at PLANCKS 2025 which will be held in Barcelona, Spain from 1-5 May, 2025

Registration Deadline - 25 th Jan, 2025
